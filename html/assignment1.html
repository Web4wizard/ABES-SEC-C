About ABES Engineering College

ABES Engineering College (ABES-EC) is one of the premier technical institutions in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh. It is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow. The college is known for academic excellence, modern infrastructure, highly qualified faculty, and strong placement opportunities.
